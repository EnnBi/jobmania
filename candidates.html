Double your job search options with us

Extend your job search and find the right role for you, whether it’s on-site or remote work. Unlike job boards or other staffing firms, Job Mania gives you two ways to advance your career. Our local talent solutions specialists promote you to employers, and you can also communicate one-on-one with hiring managers through Job Mania Direct, our online hiring platform.

It’s not a one-size-fits-all world. We offer a variety of opportunities so you can find the role and work arrangement that’s right for you.

Why Choose Us?

Why allow Job Mania Recruiting to represent you?

  • Distinction – It is no accident that such a high volume of A+ candidates every day allow recruiting consultants at Job Mania to present their resume and individual value story to the hiring managers of our clients.
  • Prestige – Job Mania Recruiting places our candidates only at respected industry-leading companies. Because we only succeed if you succeed, we have every motiviation to get you into the right position.
  • Determination – Job Mania recruiters are charged with not only getting our candidates an interview, but getting them hired! We are never pushy and always here to help you.
  • Urgency – The sooner you are in contact with one of our recruiters, the faster we can get you in touch with an opportunity you have been waiting for. We get that the time is now to change your life.
